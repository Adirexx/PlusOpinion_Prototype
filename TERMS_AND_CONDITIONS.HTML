3. Reputation Quality Score (RQS™) & Anti-Gaming Policy

PlusOpinion utilizes a proprietary Reputation Quality Score (RQS) algorithm to maintain platform integrity. Your RQS (0-100) is a weighted metric derived from four foundational pillars:

  • Consistency Score: Evaluates your reliability through active weekly participation and interaction streaks within the community.
  • Verification Credibility: Measures the accuracy and authenticity of purchase proofs provided for your verified opinions.
  • Community Impact: Quantifies the quantitative and qualitative value of your contributions, including 'Agrees' and constructive community engagement.
  • Legacy History: Assesses historical adherence to platform standards, account age, and overall trust signals.

Anti-Gaming & Manipulation

Strictly prohibited: Artificially inflating RQS, generating fake "Agrees," using bot farms, or operating multiple accounts to "game" the monetization system. Any attempt to manipulate these metrics will result in an immediate and permanent ban without notice.
