Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026. Last Updated: February 11, 2026.

1. Our Commitment: Zero Data Sale

PlusOpinion is built on trust. We operate with a fundamental principle: Your data is not a product. We do not, and will never, sell your personal information, browsing history, or opinion data to third-party data brokers or marketing agencies.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and strictly protect your information when you interact with the PlusOpinion platform. We prioritize security and transparency in every line of code we write.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Identification

During onboarding, we collect your full name, email address, chosen username, and profile bio. If you apply for the Partner Program, we collect government-issued ID details and bank account information exclusively for identity verification and payout purposes.

B. Opinion & Verification Data

We store the text of your **opinions**, images, and any proofs of purchase (receipts/invoices) you provide for verification. Purchase proofs are used solely to validate the authenticity of your experience and are never shared publicly.

C. Technical & Usage Metrics

To maintain our RQS™ (Reputation Quality Score) and prevent system gaming, we collect metadata including IP addresses, device identifiers, and platform interaction logs (likes, agrees, comments, and streaks).

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is utilized strictly for platform functionality, security, and the integrity of the ecosystem:

Conditional Collection & Data Minimization

To ensure maximal privacy, PlusOpinion does NOT collect government-issued IDs or bank details during initial onboarding. Document collection is triggered ONLY upon meeting the Partner Program eligibility thresholds.

Threshold Required

75+ RQS and 500+ Verified Interactions.

Security Warning

Report any premature ID requests to support@plusopinion.com.

RQS™ Calculation

Processing interaction quality and streaks to maintain your reputation score.

Monetization audits

Verifying eligibility and processing payouts through encrypted financial logs.

Platform Integrity

Detecting fraud and manipulation to ensure a fair ecosystem for all participants.

Smart Communications

Delivering interaction alerts and critical account security updates via encrypted channels.

Standard Data: Email, Username, Display Name
Collected at Onboarding

4. Data Retention & Compliance Archiving

We adhere to strict data retention policies to balance user privacy with platform accountability:

The Compliance Archive

When you delete an **opinion** or your account, certain data is moved to our secondary "Compliance Archive." This is required to prevent "Review-and-Delete" gaming, assist in legal investigations (such as defamation claims), and maintain the integrity of our verified history. This archived data is inaccessible to other users and is protected by encrypted access controls.

5. Enterprise-Grade Security

PlusOpinion employs multiple layers of security to safeguard your information:

  • Row Level Security (RLS): Strict database-level policies ensuring users can only access their authorized data.
  • End-to-End Encryption: All sensitive documents and identification proofs are encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • JWT Authentication: Secure, time-limited session management for all account actions.
  • Continuous Auditing: Automated security monitoring to detect and block suspicious access patterns.

6. Your Privacy Rights

We empower you with full control over your digital footprint:

Right to Access

View all data we hold about your account.

Right to Rectify

Update or correct your personal profile info.

Right to Erasure

Request account deletion (subject to archiving laws).
